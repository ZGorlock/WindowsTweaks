Launch: RegEdit
Navigate to: 'HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\WindowsRuntime\ActivatableClassId'
Right click the key: 'Windows.Gaming.GameBar.PresenceServer.Internal.PresenceWriter'
	Select 'Permissions...'
	Click 'Advanced'
	Change the Owner:
		Click 'Change' next to Owner
		Click 'Advanced'
		Click 'Find Now'
		Double click the entry for your user account
		Click 'Ok'
	Click the Permission entry for your user account
		Click 'Edit'
		Check 'Full Control'
		Click 'Ok'
	Click 'Ok'
Click 'Apply'
Click 'Ok'

Run 'disable xbox gamebar and dvr.reg'
