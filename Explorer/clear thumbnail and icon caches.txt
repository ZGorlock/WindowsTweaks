This will delete the Windows thumbnail cache and icon cache
	The caches will be rebuilt as needed by Windows Explorer

This will automatically restart the computer after running
	Ensure your work is saved and your programs are closed

Run 'clear thumbnail and icon caches.bat' as Admin
