ShellMenuView:
	Enable or disable Windows and program right click menu entries
	Made by Nir Sofer
	https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/shell_menu_view.html

ShellExView:
	Enable or disable Windows and program right click menu entries (Different options than ShMnView)
	Made by Nir Sofer
	https://www.nirsoft.net/utils/shexview.html

ShellNewHandler:
	Enable or disable options from the right click New menu
	Made by einsundnull
	https://sourceforge.net/projects/shellnewhandler/

Navigate to 'Tools'
Run 'ShellMenuView\shmnview.exe', 'ShellExView\shexview.exe', or 'ShellNewHandler\ShellNewHandler.exe'
Disable the menu options you do not want
