Navigate to 'Google Chrome'

Open Google Chrome
Browse to: chrome://extensions/

Drag 'Custom Theme.crx' to the browser
Select 'Add theme'

You can customize the theme by editing 'Custom Theme\manifest.json'
	Every time you pack the extension increment manifest_version

To pack the extension:
	Browse to: chrome://extensions/
	Select 'Pack extension'
	For the 'Extension root directory' choose the 'Custom Theme' folder
