Navigate to 'Simplify3D'

Copy 'Simplify3D-DarkTheme.qss' to 'C:\Program Files\Simplify3D-4.1.2\'
Right click the Simplify 3D shortcut
	Select 'Properties'
	Edit Target to end with: " -stylesheet Simplify3D-DarkTheme.qss"
	Click Ok
